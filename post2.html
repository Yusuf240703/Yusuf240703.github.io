Creating a Solar-Powered Light

I designed and built a solar-powered lighting device with the intent of making it as simple to produce as possible.

This design brief led me to 3D printing the device using PLA, which is a widely available plastic. The light can be hung up, for example, on a washing line to be charged. It can then be brought in and placed in the stand. Underneath the extendable panels, there are mirrors to reflect the light down and outwards.

I iterated the design in Solidworks, adding cutouts for the electrical wiring. A battery was used as a backup to the solar panels, and the panels were connected to the LED lights.
